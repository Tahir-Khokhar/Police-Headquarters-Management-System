System Features

Explore the comprehensive features designed to manage every aspect of police personnel operations

🏛️

Department Management

Organize and manage multiple police departments with their respective stations and locations. Each department can have multiple stations under its jurisdiction.

📍

Station Management

Manage police stations within each department, including location details. Stations are organized hierarchically under their parent departments.

⭐

Rank Hierarchy

Maintain a structured rank system with sequence ordering. Each rank has a unique code and title, enabling clear career progression tracking.

👤

Personnel Records

Complete personnel profiles including personal details, department assignment, current rank, and service history. Track years of service automatically.

📜

Rank History

Maintain a complete history of rank promotions for each personnel member, including start and end dates for each rank held.

📝

Application Processing

Handle applications for both Fighter and Soldier career paths. Track application status from draft through submission, review, and final approval.

✅

Intermediate Clearing

Manage the intermediate clearing process required for soldier candidates. Track clearing status and maintain notes for each personnel member.

🎓

Training Management

Assign training programs to applicants with start and end dates. Track training status from planned through enrolled to completed.

📈

Rank Upgrade System

Personnel can request rank upgrades with automatic validation of service requirements (5+ years). Track requests through recommendation to approval.

⚖️

Upgrade Decisions

Administrators can review and decide on upgrade requests, recording the final rank awarded and decision notes for transparency.

🛡️

Duty Assignment

Assign personnel to specific stations after training completion and upgrade approval. Track duty status and assignment history.

📊

Admin Dashboard

Comprehensive dashboard showing system-wide statistics including personnel on duty, training status, and application overview.
